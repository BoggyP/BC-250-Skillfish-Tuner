# Killfish Tuner 2.0 Release Notes

Killfish Tuner 2.0 is a major update focused on making BC-250 management clearer, safer, and more reliable.

## Highlights

- Improved dashboard with system information
- Cleaner CU management
- Dynamic CU gauge
- Persistent fan settings
- Fan calibration and restore
- Service health improvements
- Last-good configuration restore
- System report generation
- Benchmark subsystem removed for a cleaner core app

## Upgrade Notes

Users should review their current CU, fan, and overclocking settings after upgrading.

If using persistent CU or fan settings, verify that the relevant services are enabled and working after reboot.

## Safety

As always, validate your configuration carefully and monitor temperatures, power draw, and stability after applying changes.
